01 · LOGO

Wordmark & monogram

—Nav: wordmark at 13px height; footer at 11px
—Black on light, white (inverted) on #0a0a0a
—Monogram "SA" only for avatars & favicons
—Never recolor, skew, outline, or add effects

06 · SIGNATURE MOTIF

The bar field

Vertical 3px bars, 7px gap, varying height (35–95%) and opacity (.25–.85). Black bars hang from the top-left; orange (#e8541e) bars rise from the bottom-right. Use at section edges and as the texture behind dark closers — never centered, never as a chart.

07 · VOICE & RULES

Write like the product works

Voice
—Declarative, lowercase-calm: "Audit the funnel. Then put it on autopilot."
—Numbers over adjectives — AED figures, percentages, timings
—Em-dash asides; periods in headlines; no exclamation marks
—"System", "engine", "owns it" — never "solutions" or "synergy"
Hard rules
—No gradients, no emoji, no icon libraries — arrows (→) only
—Max 2 background colors per surface
—One orange metric per data view, max
—Squares stay sharp; interactive elements are pills
Applying to new surfaces
Social posts: #0a0a0a or #fbfbfa ground, wordmark small, one headline in 600 / -.03em, bar-field edge, orange square as the only color. Decks: one dark closer slide per deck, section eyebrows with the 7px square, window-chrome frames for any product shot. Sites & portals: this page's landing layout is the reference implementation.
